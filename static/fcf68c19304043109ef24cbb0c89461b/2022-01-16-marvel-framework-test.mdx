# Background Setup
To boostrap, we need to set the background of this universe (maybe even a parallel universe that could have connection to Marvel if later we got famous). Once you set it as an universe with so many aliens, it brings so much flexibility to connect across different titles and make your story logical and convincing. With such background, you can easily bring all heroes & heorines together to fight evil.

Talking about evil, we need a big villain and a mainline story to fight with this evil. But we have experience that big villain need to have their own story to make it real. How about `Thinos`?

> 宇宙中，有一个早期的III级文明种族，Elysian。他们最早开始银河系的文明扩张，在扩张的过程中难免会有其他文明的冲突与融合。Elysian在鼎盛时期几乎占据了银河系80%的可居住星系。他们有高度的文明，采用一种类似分布式与中央集权的混合制度，是银河系令人敬畏的最高级文明。这个文明在外看上去很难用单一的思维去理解，因为治理的广度，他们对不同的地域的扩张采取不同的激进程度。
