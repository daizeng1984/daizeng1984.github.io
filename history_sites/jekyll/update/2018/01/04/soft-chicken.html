Some Ideas

There are mainly 2 theories that I find making sense based on my experience:

  • Cook the chicken slowly and precisely control the temperature of cooking. This make sense because Onsen Tamago mainly rely on this technique.
  • Brining and marinating the chicken in salty/sugary water to break the protein structure (you might think of it as some type of cook) so that this is not normal chicken meat anymore . I did this before and it does work. One pros for this technique is that I like meat to be well done in most cases.
