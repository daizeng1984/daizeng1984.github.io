About Work

I’m currently a sr. research engineer in Visual Computing Group @ Bosch Research, Palo Alto, CA. Before I joined Bosch in 2015, I worked on web dev and services in Product & Service Discovery Group @ Amazon where I spent a great time with enthusiastic colleagues.

My earlier industrial experience started with Ubisoft back in 2008 where I worked as a software developer for Rayman HD. I have computer science, statistics and mathematics background and thus a “mixture” of geeky/nerdy things.

Even though my job already has 80% of time for coding, I’m still loving it in my spare time. I just wanna keep learning and building interesting things.
